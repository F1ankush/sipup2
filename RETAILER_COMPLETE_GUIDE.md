## â“ FAQ

### About Shopping

**Q: Can I save products for later?**
A: Currently, you need to add them to cart. We recommend making a list of needed products and adding them together.

**Q: Can I apply bulk discounts?**
A: Yes! Contact our sales team for bulk order pricing. Usually available for orders above certain quantity.

**Q: Are prices updated in real-time?**
A: Yes, prices shown are current. However, final price might change if stock is low.

---

### About Cart

**Q: How long does my cart stay saved?**
A: Your cart is saved in your session. If you close browser without checkout, cart is cleared. Login again to see previous cart.

**Q: Can I have multiple carts?**
A: No, you have one cart per account. Add all items to one cart before checkout.

**Q: Is there a maximum quantity I can add?**
A: Maximum quantity is limited by available stock. Add quantity up to stock available.

---

### About Checkout

**Q: Can I modify order after placing it?**
A: Yes, if order is still in "Pending" status. Go to order details and click "Modify Order" if available.

**Q: What if I selected wrong payment method?**
A: Contact support immediately. We can help change payment method before processing.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, if order hasn't been packed yet. Go to order details and click "Cancel Order" if option available.

---

### About Payment

**Q: What if UPI payment fails?**
A: Try again immediately. You can retry payment from order details page. Contact support if payment is deducted but not confirmed.

**Q: Do you accept other payment methods?**
A: Currently only COD and UPI. More methods coming soon.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted connections. Your payment data is protected.

---

### About Delivery

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Typically 2-5 business days depending on location and order size.

**Q: Do you deliver to my area?**
A: Check delivery address validation during checkout. If address is rejected, contact support.

**Q: What if package is damaged?**
A: Report immediately with photos. We offer replacement or refund for damaged products.

**Q: Can I change delivery address?**
A: Yes, if order is still in "Pending" status. Contact support with new address.

---

### About Invoices & Bills

**Q: When can I download invoice?**
A: After payment is confirmed. For COD, after delivery is marked as delivered.

**Q: Can I get invoice in GST format?**
A: Yes, our invoice includes GST details and is GST-compliant.

**Q: How long are invoices available?**
A: Invoices are available for download from order page permanently.
